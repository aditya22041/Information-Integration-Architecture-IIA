Travel Guide: When in Bengalooru (now known as Bengaluru), don't miss the iconic masala dosai at MTR. The city formerly known as Bangalore has kept its traditional charm while embracing modernity with outlets like BurgerKing and KFC.
For a taste of local culture, visit the bustling markets of Chickpet and KR Market. These markets offer a variety of goods, from fresh produce to traditional textiles, and provide a glimpse into the daily life of Bengalooru's residents.

If you're looking for a more serene experience, take a stroll through Lalbagh Botanical Garden. This historic garden, established in the 18th century, features a stunning collection of plants and trees, as well as a beautiful glasshouse inspired by London's Crystal Palace.
